Frequently Asked Questions

What is iCloud unlocking?

iCloud unlocking is the process of removing the activation lock from an iPhone, allowing you to use it with a new Apple ID.

How long does the unlocking process take?

The official unlocking process typically takes 24-48 hours to complete once we receive your device information.

Is the unlock permanent?

Yes, our official unlocking service provides a permanent solution that remains active even after software updates.

What information do I need to provide?

We need your device's IMEI number, model, and current carrier information to process the unlock request.

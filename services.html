Frequently Asked Questions

Is the unlock permanent?

Yes, all our unlocking services provide permanent solutions that survive iOS updates and factory resets. Once your device is unlocked, it remains unlocked forever.

How long does the unlocking process take?

Our standard processing time is 24-48 hours for most services. Some complex cases might take up to 72 hours, but we always keep you updated on the progress.

What information do you need from me?

We primarily need your device's IMEI number, which you can find by dialing *#06# on your iPhone or going to Settings > General > About. For some services, we might need additional information which will be requested during the process.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes, all our services come with a 100% money-back guarantee. If we cannot successfully unlock your device, we will refund your payment in full.

Will unlocking affect my warranty?

No, our unlocking methods are official and do not void your device's warranty. Your iPhone will continue to function normally with all features intact.

Can you unlock all iPhone models?

Yes, our services support all iPhone models from iPhone 5s to the latest iPhone 16 Pro Max, running any iOS version.
